TRANSFORMERS

DECEPTICONS

The Decepticons are a faction of Cybertronians who formed on Cybertron, They fight to destroy the heroic faction The Autobots. The Decepticons were founded by Megatron some time later, and he would start the Cybertronian War.

AUTOBOTS

The Autobots are one of two factions in the Cybertronian War. They fight to protect humans from the evil The Decepticons.The Autobots' leader is optimus Prime.
